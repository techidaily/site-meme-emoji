Place the Footage onto the Timeline

The media files you import into your projects in Filmora are located in the softwares Media Tab, and you can just drag them from the Media Tab and drop them onto a video track on the timeline. Choose an Emoji

You can then adjust its duration, position on the screen or size. Optionally, you can double-click on the emoji to access the Motion tab that enables you to choose between different motion effects.

Export the Project
